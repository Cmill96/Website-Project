Nutrition Basics

Nutrition does not have to be complicated. Most beginners do well by focusing on simple, whole foods and eating regular meals throughout the day.

Sample Daily Goals

  • Include a source of protein in each meal.
  • Fill half your plate with fruits or vegetables.
  • Drink water regularly during the day.

Beginner Nutrition Table

This table gives quick examples of foods in each major nutrient group.

Nutrient Why It Helps Beginner-Friendly Foods
Protein Supports muscle repair and keeps you full. Chicken, Greek yogurt, eggs, beans
Carbohydrates Gives your body energy for workouts and daily life. Rice, oats, potatoes, whole-grain bread
Healthy Fats Helps with hormone balance and brain function. Avocado, nuts, olive oil, peanut butter
Hydration Improves performance and recovery. Water, low-sugar sports drinks, herbal tea
