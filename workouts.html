Beginner Workout Guide

These workouts are designed for people who are just starting out. The focus is on learning good form, staying consistent, and not doing too much too soon.

Weekly Workout Plan

  • Day 1: Full body routine (light weights or bodyweight)
  • Day 2: Rest or light walk
  • Day 3: Upper body routine
  • Day 4: Rest or stretching
  • Day 5: Lower body routine
  • Day 6: Optional light cardio
  • Day 7: Rest
