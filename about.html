About This Project

This website was created as a beginner-friendly guide to fitness and nutrition. The goal is to give new lifters and people who are just starting to work out a simple place to learn the basics.

The content focuses on clear explanations, short workout examples, and realistic nutrition tips that can fit into a normal schedule.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Simple workout routines that don’t require much equipment.
  • Basic nutrition suggestions with everyday foods.
  • Ideas to stay consistent without feeling overwhelmed.
